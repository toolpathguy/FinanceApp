# Design Document: Accounts Management Page

## Status: Placeholder

## Overview

Create a dedicated accounts management page for adding, editing, and organizing real financial accounts (checking, savings, credit cards). Currently accounts are implicitly created through journal transactions — this page would give users explicit control.

## Areas to Address

- List all real accounts (assets + liabilities) with current balances
- Add new account (name, type, opening balance)
- Account reconciliation workflow
- Credit card payment tracking (link CC to checking, show payment due)
- Account transfer UI (move money between real accounts, with envelope implications)
- Account closing/archiving
- Multi-currency support considerations
- Integration with YNAB Rule 1 (all accounts participate in envelope system)
